About Myself

I am an assistant professor in Department of Multimedia Engineering, Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, Osaka Univ, Japan (Fujiwara lab). My research focuces on theoretical aspects of cryptography, including public key encryption schemes, zero-knowledge proof systems, signature schemes and so on. I obtained my Ph.D. in cryptography from Kyoto University in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Abe and Prof. Tibouchi (Abe-Tibouchi lab). See also [dblp] and [google scholar].
