About Myself

I'm am working at the Cyber Physical Security Research Center, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST), as a postdoctoral researcher. My research focuces on theoretical aspects of cryptography, including public key encryption schemes, zero-knowledge proof systems, signature schemes and so on. I was a PhD student at Abe-Tibouchi lab, Kyoto university, Japan, with Masayuki Abe and Mehdi Tibouchi as my supervisors. See also [dblp].
